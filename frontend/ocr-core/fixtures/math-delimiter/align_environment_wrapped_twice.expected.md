Mathpix sometimes splits the aligned derivation of the luminosity distance with nested delimiters:

$$
\begin{align}
D_L(z) &= (1+z)c\int_0^z \frac{dz'}{H(z')}\\
&= \frac{c(1+z)}{H_0}\int_0^z \frac{dz'}{\sqrt{\Omega_m(1+z')^3+\Omega_\Lambda}}.
\end{align}
$$

This form is used for the supernova Hubble diagram.
